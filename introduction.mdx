# Welcome to Meteroid

Meteroid is a cloud-native pricing and billing infrastructure designed specifically for product-led SaaS companies. It bridges the gap between customer usage and billing, enabling accurate, transparent, and flexible usage-based pricing models.

<img
  src="/images/meteroid-banner.png"
  alt="Meteroid Platform"
  width="640"
/>

## Why Meteroid?

Meteroid addresses the complexities and limitations of traditional billing systems, particularly for businesses transitioning to usage-based models or product-led-growth principles. It eliminates the gap between customer usage and billing, ensuring accuracy and transparency.

## How It Works

Meteroid integrates with your existing systems via a simple API, collecting data on customer usage and interactions. This data fuels the Meteroid billing engine, applying your custom pricing models to generate accurate, timely invoices.

<img
  src="/images/meteroid-schema-4.webp"

  width="640"
/>

### The Meteroid Platform

1. **Metering**: Ingest usage events via API or Kafka. Track any metric with support for aggregations, dimensions, and filters.

2. **Billing**: Define pricing plans with multiple components. Manage customers, subscriptions, and invoices through the API or web interface.

3. **Invoicing**: Automatically generate invoices based on usage and subscriptions. Handle credits, discounts, taxes, and payment collection.

## Who Is Meteroid For?

Meteroid is designed for startups, SMEs, and enterprises looking to embrace:

- **Usage-based billing** - Charge customers based on actual consumption
- **Product-led growth** - Enable self-service pricing and checkout
- **Re-invoicing** - Bill customers for third-party service consumption
- **Complex pricing** - Multi-dimensional pricing with tiered rates
